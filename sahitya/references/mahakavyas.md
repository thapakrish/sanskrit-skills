# Mahakavyas (Epic Poems)

## Definition of Mahakavya

According to Dandin's Kavyadarsha, a mahakavya should have:
- **सर्गबन्ध**: Division into sargas (cantos)
- **आशीर्नमस्क्रिया**: Blessing or salutation at opening
- **वस्तुनिर्देश**: Indication of subject matter
- **नायक**: Noble hero (usually a god or king)
- **रस**: Dominant rasa (शृङ्गार or वीर)
- **छन्द**: Varied meters, one meter per sarga
- **सन्ध्या/ऋतु/नगर वर्णन**: Descriptions of twilight, seasons, cities
- **उदयास्त**: Sunrise/sunset descriptions
- **उद्यान/जलक्रीडा/मधुपान**: Garden, water-play, drinking scenes

## रघुवंशम् (Raghuvamsha)

**Author**: कालिदास
**Sargas**: 19
**Meter varies by sarga**
**Rasa**: वीर (heroic) with शृङ्गार

## कुमारसम्भवम् (Kumarasambhava)

**Author**: कालिदास
**Sargas**: 17 (sargas 9-17 disputed)
**Rasa**: शृङ्गार

### Sarga-by-Sarga Summary

| Sarga | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Description of Himalaya; birth of Parvati |
| 2 | Narada visits; prophecy of Parvati's marriage to Shiva |
| 3 | Parvati's tapas to win Shiva |
| 4 | Brahma sends Kama to disturb Shiva's meditation |
| 5 | Kama burned by Shiva's third eye; Rati's lament |
| 6 | Parvati continues tapas; Shiva tests her as brahmachari |
| 7 | Shiva reveals himself; agrees to marry |
| 8 | Wedding of Shiva and Parvati |
| (9-17) | Wedding night; birth of Kartikeya (disputed authorship) |

### Famous Verses

**1.1**:
> अस्त्युत्तरस्यां दिशि देवतात्मा हिमालयो नाम नगाधिराजः।
> पूर्वापरौ तोयनिधी वगाह्य स्थितः पृथिव्या इव मानदण्डः॥

## किरातार्जुनीयम् (Kiratarjuniya)

**Author**: भारवि (6th century)
**Sargas**: 18
**Rasa**: वीर (heroic)
**Style**: अर्थगौरव (weighty meaning)

### Sarga-by-Sarga Summary

| Sarga | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1-2 | Pandavas in exile; Draupadi urges action |
| 3-4 | Yudhishthira's dilemma; counsel for war |
| 5-6 | Arjuna leaves for Indrakila; journey description |
| 7-10 | Forest descriptions; Arjuna's tapas |
| 11-12 | Wild boar (demon Muka) appears |
| 13-14 | Arjuna and Kirata (Shiva in disguise) both shoot boar |
| 15-16 | Battle between Arjuna and Kirata |
| 17 | Arjuna recognizes Shiva; receives Pashupata weapon |
| 18 | Shiva blesses Arjuna |

### Famous Verse (15.14 - Citrakavya)

> न नोननुन्नो नुन्नोनो नाना नानानना ननु।
> नुन्नोऽनुन्नो ननुन्नेनो नानेना नुन्ननुन्ननुत्॥

(Uses only 'न' consonant — an extreme example of anuprasa)

## शिशुपालवधम् (Shishupalavadha)

**Author**: माघ (7th century)
**Sargas**: 20
**Rasa**: वीर with शृङ्गार
**Style**: Combines भारवि's depth + कालिदास's sweetness

### Sarga-by-Sarga Summary

| Sarga | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Narada visits Dvaraka; describes Yudhishthira's rajasuya |
| 2-3 | Krishna's court; preparation for journey |
| 4 | Krishna's journey; twilight description |
| 5-6 | Night, moonrise, city of Indraprastha |
| 7-8 | Krishna meets Yudhishthira; garden descriptions |
| 9 | Rajasuya yajna; offerings to guests |
| 10 | Question of first honor; Bhishma recommends Krishna |
| 11 | Shishupala's rage; hundred insults |
| 12-14 | Shishupala's provocations continue |
| 15-16 | Krishna's patience; Bhima's anger |
| 17-18 | War preparations; army descriptions |
| 19 | Sudarshana chakra released |
| 20 | Shishupala killed; merges into Krishna |

### Famous Verse (2.10)

> उपकारिषु यः साधुः साधुत्वे तस्य को विशेषः।
> अपकारिषु यः साधुः स साधुः सद्भिरुच्यते॥

## नैषधीयचरितम् (Naishadhiyacharita)

**Author**: श्रीहर्ष (12th century)
**Sargas**: 22
**Rasa**: शृङ्गार
**Style**: Most difficult of the five; complex language

### Sarga-by-Sarga Summary

| Sarga | Content |
|-------|---------|
| 1-2 | Damayanti's beauty; Nala's virtue |
| 3-4 | Hamsa carries message between them |
| 5-6 | Gods desire Damayanti; send Nala as messenger |
| 7-8 | Nala enters Damayanti's chamber invisibly |
| 9-10 | Svayamvara announced; gods disguise as Nala |
| 11-12 | Damayanti identifies real Nala |
| 13-14 | Marriage; Kali's curse |
| 15-17 | Nala loses kingdom gambling |
| 18-19 | Exile; separation from Damayanti |
| 20-22 | Nala's transformation; reunion (incomplete) |

## Comparative Analysis

| Feature | रघुवंश | कुमारसम्भव | किरातार्जुनीय | शिशुपालवध | नैषधीय |
|---------|--------|------------|---------------|------------|---------|
| **Sargas** | 19 | 17 | 18 | 20 | 22 |
| **Primary Rasa** | वीर | शृङ्गार | वीर | वीर | शृङ्गार |
| **Style** | माधुर्य | माधुर्य | अर्थगौरव | Mixed | Complex |
| **Difficulty** | Medium | Medium | Hard | Hard | Hardest |
| **Best for** | Beginners | Beginners | Advanced | Advanced | Scholars |

### Traditional Saying

> उपमा कालिदासस्य भारवेरर्थगौरवम्।
> दण्डिनः पदलालित्यं माघे सन्ति त्रयो गुणाः॥

"Kalidasa's similes, Bharavi's depth, Dandin's word-grace — Magha has all three."
