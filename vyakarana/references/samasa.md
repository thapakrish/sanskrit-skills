# Samasa (समास) - Compound Types

## Overview

समास = सम् (together) + √अस् (to throw/place)

A compound (समास) combines two or more words into a single unit, dropping the case endings of all but the final member.

**Key terms:**
- **पूर्वपद**: First/prior member
- **उत्तरपद**: Last/final member
- **समस्तपद**: The compound word
- **विग्रह**: Dissolution (analysis into component words)

---

## Main Types of Samasa

### 1. अव्ययीभाव (Avyayibhava)
**"Becoming indeclinable"** — First member is an indeclinable (अव्यय), compound functions as adverb.

| Compound | Vigraha | Meaning |
|----------|---------|---------|
| उपकृष्णम् | कृष्णस्य समीपम् | near Krishna |
| प्रतिदिनम् | दिने दिने | daily |
| यथाशक्ति | शक्तिम् अनतिक्रम्य | according to ability |
| निर्मक्षिकम् | मक्षिकाणाम् अभावः | without flies |
| अनुरूपम् | रूपस्य योग्यम् | suitably |

**Markers**: उप, प्रति, अनु, यथा, निर्, स, etc.

**Gender**: Always neuter singular (like the indeclinable it becomes)

---

### 2. तत्पुरुष (Tatpurusha)
**"His man"** — Second member is primary; first member modifies it.

#### 2a. विभक्तितत्पुरुष (Case-based)

The first member relates to the second through an implied case relationship:

| Type | Case | Example | Vigraha |
|------|------|---------|---------|
| द्वितीया | Acc. | ग्रामगतः | ग्रामं गतः (gone to village) |
| तृतीया | Instr. | मातृसदृशः | मात्रा सदृशः (similar to mother) |
| चतुर्थी | Dat. | यूपदारु | यूपाय दारु (wood for post) |
| पञ्चमी | Abl. | ऋणमुक्तः | ऋणात् मुक्तः (freed from debt) |
| षष्ठी | Gen. | राजपुत्रः | राज्ञः पुत्रः (king's son) |
| सप्तमी | Loc. | घटोष्णः | घटे उष्णः (hot in pot) |

**षष्ठीतत्पुरुष** is most common (genitive relationship).

#### 2b. नञ्तत्पुरुष (Negative)
First member is न/अ/अन् (negative prefix)

| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| अब्राह्मणः | न ब्राह्मणः | non-brahmin |
| अनश्वः | न अश्वः | non-horse |
| अपुत्रः | न पुत्रः | without son |

#### 2c. उपपदतत्पुरुष
Second member is a verbal derivative (कृदन्त)

| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| कुम्भकारः | कुम्भं करोति यः | pot-maker |
| जलदः | जलं ददाति यः | cloud (water-giver) |
| पङ्कजम् | पङ्के जायते यत् | lotus (mud-born) |

---

### 3. कर्मधारय (Karmadharaya)
**"Holding the object"** — Both members are in same case (समानाधिकरण).

#### 3a. विशेषणपूर्वपद (Adjective-first)
| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| नीलोत्पलम् | नीलम् उत्पलम् | blue lotus |
| महाराजः | महान् राजा | great king |
| प्रियसखा | प्रियः सखा | dear friend |

#### 3b. विशेष्यपूर्वपद (Noun-first)
| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| घनश्यामः | घनः इव श्यामः | dark like a cloud |

#### 3c. उपमानपूर्वपद (Simile-first)
| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| चन्द्रमुखी | चन्द्र इव मुखं यस्याः | moon-faced |
| कमलनयना | कमलम् इव नयने | lotus-eyed |

#### 3d. उपमानोत्तरपद (Simile-second)
| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| पुरुषव्याघ्रः | पुरुषः व्याघ्रः इव | man-tiger (heroic man) |
| मुखचन्द्रः | मुखं चन्द्रः इव | face-moon |

---

### 4. द्विगु (Dvigu)
**Numerical compound** — First member is a number.

| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| पञ्चवटी | पञ्चानां वटानां समाहारः | group of five banyan trees |
| त्रिलोकी | त्रयाणां लोकानां समाहारः | three worlds |
| सप्ताहः | सप्तानाम् अहानां समाहारः | week |
| शताब्दी | शतं अब्दानाम् | century |

**Often becomes feminine with ई suffix.**

---

### 5. द्वन्द्व (Dvandva)
**"Pair"** — Copulative compound (A and B).

#### 5a. इतरेतरद्वन्द्व (Distributive)
Each member retains individuality; dual/plural ending.

| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| रामलक्ष्मणौ | रामश्च लक्ष्मणश्च | Rama and Lakshmana |
| पाणिपादम् | पाणी च पादौ च | hands and feet |
| अहोरात्रम् | अहश्च रात्रिश्च | day and night |
| मातापितरौ | माता च पिता च | mother and father |

#### 5b. समाहारद्वन्द्व (Collective)
Members form a collective unit; neuter singular.

| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| पाणिपादम् | पाण्योः पादयोश्च समाहारः | hands and feet (as a set) |
| आहारनिद्रा | आहारश्च निद्रा च | food and sleep |

---

### 6. बहुव्रीहि (Bahuvrihi)
**"Much rice"** — Exocentric compound; describes an external referent.

The compound as a whole is an adjective describing something/someone else.

| Example | Vigraha | Meaning |
|---------|---------|---------|
| बहुव्रीहिः | बहवः व्रीहयः यस्य सः | one who has much rice |
| नीलकण्ठः | नीलः कण्ठः यस्य सः | one whose throat is blue (Shiva) |
| चक्रपाणिः | चक्रं पाणौ यस्य सः | one who has discus in hand (Vishnu) |
| चतुर्भुजः | चत्वारः भुजाः यस्य सः | four-armed one |
| पीताम्बरः | पीतम् अम्बरं यस्य सः | yellow-clothed one (Krishna/Vishnu) |
| दशाननः | दश आननानि यस्य सः | ten-faced one (Ravana) |
| लम्बोदरः | लम्बम् उदरं यस्य सः | pot-bellied one (Ganesha) |

**Key identifier**: विग्रह includes यस्य/यस्याः/यस्य (whose) + सः/सा/तत् (he/she/it)

---

## Identifying Compound Types

```
1. Is first member अव्यय (उप, प्रति, etc.)? → अव्ययीभाव

2. Does compound describe external entity (यस्य सः)? → बहुव्रीहि

3. Is it "A and B"? → द्वन्द्व

4. Is first member a number? → द्विगु

5. Are members in same case (adjective-noun)? → कर्मधारय

6. Is there implied case between members? → तत्पुरुष
```

---

## Compound within Compound

Complex compounds can nest:
- **नीलोत्पलनयना** = (नील+उत्पल)कर्मधारय + नयन = बहुव्रीहि
  - "She whose eyes are like blue lotuses"
