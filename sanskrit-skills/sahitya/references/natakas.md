# Natakas (Sanskrit Drama)

## Dramatic Theory

### Natyashastra

The **नाट्यशास्त्र** of Bharata (c. 2nd century BCE - 2nd century CE) is the foundational text, covering:
- Rasa theory
- Stage design
- Acting techniques
- Musical modes
- Dance forms

### Structure of Drama

**Five Sandhis** (Junctures):

| Sandhi | Name | Function |
|--------|------|----------|
| 1 | मुख | Opening; seed (बीज) planted |
| 2 | प्रतिमुख | Progression; drop (बिन्दु) |
| 3 | गर्भ | Development; episode (पताका) |
| 4 | विमर्श | Pause/reversal; incident (प्रकरी) |
| 5 | निर्वहण | Denouement; conclusion (कार्य) |

### Types of Drama (Rupaka)

| Type | Acts | Hero | Plot |
|------|------|------|------|
| नाटक | 5-10 | धीरोदात्त | From known sources |
| प्रकरण | 5-10 | धीरशान्त | Invented |
| भाण | 1 | धूर्त | Monologue |
| व्यायोग | 1 | धीरोद्धत | Battle theme |
| समवकार | 3 | Multiple | Gods and demons |
| डिम | 4 | Multiple | Superhuman |
| ईहामृग | 4 | धीरोदात्त | Invented, heroic |
| अङ्क | 1 | Low born | Sorrowful |
| प्रहसन | 1 | Comic | Humorous |
| वीथी | 1 | Any | 13 elements |

## Major Dramas

### अभिज्ञानशाकुन्तलम् (Abhijnana-Shakuntala)

**Author**: कालिदास
**Acts**: 7
**Rasa**: शृङ्गार (विप्रलम्भ → सम्भोग)

#### Act-by-Act Summary

| Act | Sanskrit | Content |
|-----|----------|---------|
| 1 | अभिज्ञान | Dushyanta hunts; meets Shakuntala at Kanva's ashram |
| 2 | संकेत | Love develops; Shakuntala's friends assist |
| 3 | सम्भोग | Gandharva marriage |
| 4 | शापमोक्ष | Shakuntala cursed by Durvasa; sent to husband |
| 5 | प्रत्याख्यान | King doesn't recognize her; ring lost |
| 6 | चक्रवर्ती | Years pass; fisherman finds ring; king remembers |
| 7 | समागम | Reunion with Shakuntala and son Bharata |

#### Famous Verses

**Opening benediction**:
> या सृष्टिः स्रष्टुराद्या वहति विधिहुतं या हविर्या च होत्री।
> ये द्वे कालं विधत्तः श्रुतिविषयगुणा या स्थिता व्याप्य विश्वम्।
> यामाहुः सर्वबीजप्रकृतिरिति यया प्राणिनः प्राणवन्तः।
> प्रत्यक्षाभिः प्रपन्नस्तनुभिरवतु वस्ताभिरष्टाभिरीशः॥

### मृच्छकटिकम् (Mrichchhakatika)

**Author**: शूद्रक
**Acts**: 10
**Rasa**: शृङ्गार, करुण
**Type**: प्रकरण (invented plot)

#### Summary

Charudatta, an impoverished Brahmin, loves Vasantasena, a courtesan. Interwoven plots include political intrigue (overthrow of King Palaka) and mistaken identity (Vasantasena confused with Charudatta's wife).

**Significance**: Only major drama with a Brahmin hero who is poor; social realism; happy ending despite near-tragedy.

---

### विक्रमोर्वशीयम् (Vikramorvasiya)

**Author**: कालिदास
**Acts**: 5
**Rasa**: शृङ्गार

#### Summary

King Pururavas rescues apsara Urvashi from demons. They fall in love, but Urvashi is cursed to become a creeper if Pururavas sees her child. Eventually reunited through divine intervention.

---

### मालविकाग्निमित्रम् (Malavikagnimitra)

**Author**: कालिदास
**Acts**: 5
**Rasa**: शृङ्गार (comic elements)

Kalidasa's first play. King Agnimitra falls for Malavika, a servant. Comic complications from queens' jealousy. Malavika revealed as princess; marriage approved.

---

### मुद्राराक्षसम् (Mudrarakshasa)

**Author**: विशाखदत्त
**Acts**: 7
**Rasa**: वीर (political intrigue)

Chanakya's strategy to win over Rakshasa, minister of the defeated Nanda dynasty, to serve Chandragupta Maurya. No love interest; pure political drama.

---

### उत्तररामचरितम् (Uttararamacharita)

**Author**: भवभूति
**Acts**: 7
**Rasa**: करुण

The story after Rama's coronation: Sita's exile, birth of Lava-Kusha, eventual reunion. Known for its pathos (करुणरस).

**Famous verse** (on poetry):
> एको रसः करुण एव निमित्तभेदात्
> भिन्नः पृथक्पृथगिवाश्रयते विवर्तान्।

"There is but one rasa, karuna; it appears differently due to different causes."

---

### मालतीमाधवम् (Malatimadhava)

**Author**: भवभूति
**Acts**: 10
**Rasa**: शृङ्गार

Love story with supernatural elements: Madhava loves Malati but she is promised to another. Includes ghosts, tantric practices, and eventual happy ending.

---

### वेणीसंहारम् (Venisamhara)

**Author**: भट्टनारायण
**Acts**: 6
**Rasa**: वीर, रौद्र

Bhima's vow to avenge Draupadi's humiliation by destroying the Kauravas. Climactic killing of Duryodhana; braiding of Draupadi's hair with blood.

---

### रत्नावली (Ratnavali)

**Author**: हर्षवर्धन (or श्रीहर्ष)
**Acts**: 4
**Type**: नाटिका

King Udayana and Ratnavali. Light romantic comedy.

---

### नागानन्दम् (Nagananda)

**Author**: हर्षवर्धन
**Acts**: 5
**Rasa**: वीर, करुण (Buddhist themes)

Jimutavahana sacrifices himself to save Nagas from Garuda. Buddhist values of compassion and self-sacrifice.

## Dramatic Conventions

### Characters

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| नायक | Hero |
| नायिका | Heroine |
| विदूषक | Comic brahmin companion |
| विट | Libertine friend |
| चेटी | Maid/confidante |
| सूत्रधार | Stage manager (prologue) |
| नटी | Actress (prologue) |

### Language

- Heroes/kings speak Sanskrit
- Women and lower characters speak Prakrit
- Shaurasen for women
- Magadhi for low characters

### Stage Directions

- नेपथ्ये (offstage)
- प्रविश्य (enters)
- आत्मगतम् (aside)
- जनान्तिकम् (whispered to another)
