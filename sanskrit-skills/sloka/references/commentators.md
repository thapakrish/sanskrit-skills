# Traditional Sanskrit Commentators

## Kavya (Poetry)

### Mallinatha (मल्लिनाथः) - 14th-15th century
Primary commentator for Kalidasa's works.

**Works commented:**
- **Raghuvamsha** - Sanjivini Tika (सञ्जीविनी)
- **Kumarasambhava** - Sanjivini Tika
- **Meghaduta** - Sanjivini Tika
- **Kiratarjuniya** (Bharavi)
- **Shishupalavadha** (Magha)
- **Naishadhiyacharita** (Shriharsha)

**Style**: Grammatical analysis, word derivations, identification of alankaras

### Vallabhadeva (वल्लभदेवः) - 10th century
Earlier commentator on Kalidasa.

**Works commented:**
- Raghuvamsha - older commentary

### Hemadri (हेमाद्रिः)
**Works commented:**
- Naishadhiyacharita

## Darshana (Philosophy)

### Shankaracharya (शङ्कराचार्यः) - 8th century
Advaita Vedanta founder.

**Works commented:**
- **Bhagavad Gita** - Gita Bhashya (primary philosophical commentary)
- **Brahma Sutras** - Brahma Sutra Bhashya
- **Principal Upanishads** - Upanishad Bhashyas

### Ramanujacharya (रामानुजाचार्यः) - 11th-12th century
Vishishtadvaita school.

**Works commented:**
- **Bhagavad Gita** - Gita Bhashya
- **Brahma Sutras** - Sri Bhashya

### Madhvacharya (मध्वाचार्यः) - 13th century
Dvaita school.

**Works commented:**
- **Bhagavad Gita** - Gita Bhashya
- **Brahma Sutras** - commentary

## Purana & Bhakti

### Sridhara Swami (श्रीधरस्वामी) - 14th century
**Works commented:**
- **Bhagavata Purana** - Bhavartha Dipika (भावार्थदीपिका)
- **Vishnu Purana**

### Vallabhacharya (वल्लभाचार्यः) - 15th-16th century
Pushti Marga founder.

**Works commented:**
- **Bhagavata Purana** - Subodhini (सुबोधिनी)

### Vishvanatha Chakravarti (विश्वनाथ चक्रवर्ती) - 17th-18th century
Gaudiya Vaishnava tradition.

**Works commented:**
- **Bhagavata Purana** - Sarartha Darshini

### Jiva Goswami (जीव गोस्वामी) - 16th century
**Works commented:**
- **Bhagavata Purana** - Krama Sandarbha

## Using Commentaries

When referencing a commentary:
1. Name the commentator and their work
2. Provide the relevant grammatical or interpretive insight
3. Note any scholarly disagreements between commentators
4. Distinguish between grammatical explanation and philosophical interpretation
