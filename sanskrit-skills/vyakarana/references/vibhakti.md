# Vibhakti (विभक्ति) - Case Declensions

## Overview

Sanskrit nouns decline for:
- **विभक्ति** (Case): 7 cases + vocative
- **वचन** (Number): एकवचन, द्विवचन, बहुवचन
- **लिङ्ग** (Gender): पुंल्लिङ्ग, स्त्रीलिङ्ग, नपुंसकलिङ्ग

---

## Case Functions (Karaka System)

| विभक्ति | Name | कारक | Function | Question |
|---------|------|------|----------|----------|
| प्रथमा | Nominative | कर्ता | Subject, predicate | Who/what does? |
| द्वितीया | Accusative | कर्म | Direct object, goal | Whom/what? |
| तृतीया | Instrumental | करण | Instrument, means | By what? With whom? |
| चतुर्थी | Dative | सम्प्रदान | Recipient, purpose | To/for whom? |
| पञ्चमी | Ablative | अपादान | Source, separation | From what? |
| षष्ठी | Genitive | सम्बन्ध | Possession, relation | Of whom? |
| सप्तमी | Locative | अधिकरण | Location, time | In/on/at what? |
| सम्बोधन | Vocative | — | Address | O...! |

## Special Usages

### द्वितीया (Accusative) beyond direct object:
- **Duration**: मासं वसति (lives for a month)
- **Distance**: क्रोशं गच्छति (goes a krosa)
- **Destination**: ग्रामं गच्छति (goes to village)

### तृतीया (Instrumental) special uses:
- **Cause**: भयेन पलायते (flees from fear)
- **Accompaniment**: पुत्रेण सह (with son)
- **Comparison**: मया समः (equal to me)

### चतुर्थी (Dative) special uses:
- **Purpose**: विद्यायै तपस्यति (practices austerity for knowledge)
- **Blessing/curse**: शिवाय स्तु (may it be for Shiva/auspiciousness)

### पञ्चमी (Ablative) special uses:
- **Comparison**: रामात् बलवान् (stronger than Rama)
- **Fear**: चोरात् बिभेति (fears the thief)
- **Cause**: क्रोधात् मूढः (deluded from anger)

### षष्ठी (Genitive) special uses:
- **Partitive**: नराणां वरः (best of men)
- **With verbal nouns**: तस्य गमनम् (his going)

### सप्तमी (Locative) special uses:
- **Time**: प्रातःकाले (at dawn)
- **Condition**: सति धने (when there is wealth)
- **Object of emotion**: पुत्रे स्निह्यति (loves the son)
